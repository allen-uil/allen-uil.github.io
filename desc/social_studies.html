Social Studies consists of an MCQ test and essay, centered around a yearly upating theme & reading list (2022 has the Olympics and 2023 the Great Depression). The MCQ will typically cover questions related to the primary reading as well as general social studies term that may be recallable from APUSH, and the essay will typically relate to the annual theme.

  • 45 Questions + Essay
  • 90 minutes
  • Scoring:
    • Between +1 and +3 depending on the part the question is in, for a right answer
    • The essay is graded from a scale of 0 to 20
